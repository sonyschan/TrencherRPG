## Knowledge Base

### What is IdleTrencher?
- An idle game that visualizes your Solana wallet as an RPG party
- Each token you hold becomes a "partner" character that levels up over time
- Longer you hold = more EXP = higher level characters
- Price movements affect character animations (pumping = happy, dumping = sad)
- Completely passive - just connect wallet and watch your portfolio come to life

### Key Features
- **Partner System**: Top 10 tokens become RPG characters with levels (1-60)
- **EXP System**: 1 hour holding in top 10 = 1 EXP, Lv60 = 1 year holding
- **HP Bars**: Visual representation of gains/losses vs entry price
- **Skins**: Unlock different character appearances as you level up
- **Village Mode**: Explore other wallets as "villages" to see their portfolios

### Recent Updates (v1.8.x)
- Village Mode for exploring other wallets
- Social links integration in token dialogs
- PWA support for mobile
