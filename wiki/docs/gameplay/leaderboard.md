## How Rankings Work

Your **total score** is calculated by summing the levels of all your partner characters:

```
Total Score = Sum of All Partner Levels
```

For example:
- Partner 1: Lv15
- Partner 2: Lv12
- Partner 3: Lv8
- **Total Score: 35**
