## For Artists: Create Your Own Skin!

We're building IdleTrencher as a community project, and we'd love your contributions.

If you're a **3D artist**, you can create character skins that get added to the game. Contributors automatically get to use their own creations!

**Requirements:**
- Rigged 3D model compatible with our animation system
- Multiple animation states (walk, run, celebrate, exercise)
- Family-friendly design

Interested? Check out the [Community](/community#contribute-3d-characters) page for more details on how to contribute.
