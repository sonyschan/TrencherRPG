## EXP & Levels

Your partners gain **experience points (EXP)** just by being in your portfolio. The longer you hold, the stronger they become!

![EXP and Levels](/img/partners-exp-level.jpg)

### How EXP Works

- **1 day holding** = 1 EXP (roughly)
- **Bonus EXP**: When your token is pumping (price increasing), you gain bonus hours toward EXP
- **Max Level**: 60 (achieved after about 1 year of holding)

:::warning EXP Reset
If a partner is removed from your portfolio â€” whether you sold the token or its value dropped below other holdings â€” their EXP will be reset to zero.
:::
